I like programming! Whether it be making games, websites, or competitive programming, I have experience in all of it! I also enjoy playing games.

Here are some projects I've worked on!

Monster Hunter Paint

Microsoft Paint clone with Monster Hunter theme. Made for ICS3U midterm project (2020) with PyGame. It has features such as highlighter tool, polygon tool, stamps, a reset tool, and a music player on top of many of the default paint tools.

Trials

Original game made for final project in ICS3U (2020) with PyGame in collaboration with Yazan Hailat. A tower defense game where you fight waves of enemies to fight a boss at the end of each level. You can get weapons and fight complex AI.
